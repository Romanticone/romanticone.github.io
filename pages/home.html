About Me

Guang is a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the MIT Institute for Data, Systems, and Society, Schwarzman College of Computing, working with Prof. Alex 'Sandy' Pentland at the MIT Media Lab . He obtained his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science Under Prof. Desheng Zhang at Rutgers University. Guang is interested in finding meaningful patterns from large-scale data based on data-driven modeling and then designing socially informed decision making services to enhance systems the data generated. He is good at big data analytics and machine learning. Currently, his research is mainly focusing on Cyber-Physical Systems, Big Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and Human-Centered Computing, especially for applications on Smart Cities and Human Mobility. He has been publishing extensively in top-tier Computer Science conferences including ACM IMWUT/UbiComp, ACM MobiCom, ACM SIGKDD, IEEE RTSS, IEEE ICDE, and WWW,etc.
