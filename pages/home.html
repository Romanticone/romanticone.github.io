About Me

Guang is a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science Under Prof. Desheng Zhang at Rutgers University. Before coming to Rutgers, he obtained the MS degree with honor at the State Key Laboratory of Rail Traffic Control and Safety, Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China in 2017, where he was lucky to meet some talented researchers and started his academic career. Guang is interested in big data analytics, machine learning, and large-scale cyber-physical systems. Currently, his research is focusing on vehicle mobility and charging scheduling of heterogeneous electric vehicle networks including taxi, bus, car sharing, personal vehicle, etc.
