About Me

Guang is a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science Under Prof. Desheng Zhang at Rutgers University. He is also working with working with Prof. Alex 'Sandy' Pentland at MIT for the Connection Science Program, which aims to provide groundbreaking insights into human behavior and foster innovation to improve lives. Guang is interested in data-driven research, e.g., finding meaningful patterns from data and then designing applications to enhance systems the data generated. He is good at big data analytics and artificial intelligence. Currently, his research is mainly focusing on the Data Science, Data-Driven Cyber-Physical Systems, Computational Social Science, and Artificial Intelligence, especially for applications in Smart Cities and Human Behavior. His technical contributions have led to more than 30 papers in peer-reviewed publications.
